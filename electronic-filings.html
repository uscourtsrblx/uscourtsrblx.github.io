Electronic Filing System

NOTICE: CJIS WARNING

You are accessing a restricted infromation system. System usage may be monitored, recorded, and subject to audit. Unauthorized use of the system is prohibited and may be subject to criminal and/or civil penalties. Use of the system indicates consent to monitoring and recording.

The Clerk of the Supreme Court of The United States is responsible for handling all filings and documents that are addressed to the Court or its members. The E-Filing Form is used to send documents addressed to the Court and is open all hours of the day.
