Orders Lists

The Supreme Court will publish an orders list frequently to outline which cases it will hear and which cases it rejects. Most of these orders come without any comment from the Court except when they feature an opinion related to the order. On rarer occasions, the Supreme Court or an individual justice may release a Miscellaneous Order that regards an individual case, typically to adjudicate an application made to the Court or individual justice for that case.
