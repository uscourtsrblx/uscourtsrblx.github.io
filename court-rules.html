Public Releases

The Public Information Office of the State Supreme Court is responsible for making all press releases related to the Court's business and day-to-day operations as well as making media advisories to members of the press in certain situations which require special attention, such as high-profile cases where entry to the press will be limited.

For media contact or other information, please contact Public Information Officer Stanley_Labson at Stanlabs#0978.
